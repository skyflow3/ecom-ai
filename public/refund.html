Refund Policy

Last updated: May 14, 2026

60-Day Money-Back Guarantee. If you're not completely satisfied with your purchase, we'll give you a full refund within 60 days of delivery — no questions asked.

1. Eligibility

To be eligible for a refund:

  • Your item must be unused and in the same condition you received it
  • The request must be made within 60 days of delivery
  • You must provide your order number

2. How to Request a Refund

Email us at contact@nutrovia.co with:

  • Your order number
  • The reason for the return
  • Whether you'd like a refund or exchange

3. Refund Process

Once your refund request is approved:

  • We will process the refund within 5-7 business days
  • The refund will be credited to your original payment method
  • Depending on your bank, it may take an additional 5-10 days to appear on your statement

4. Partial Refunds

Partial refunds may be granted in the following cases:

  • Item returned after the 60-day window (at our discretion)
  • Item opened or used but with a valid quality concern

5. Damaged or Defective Items

If you receive a damaged or defective product, contact us immediately at contact@nutrovia.co with photos of the issue. We will send a replacement at no cost.

6. Contact

For any refund questions, email contact@nutrovia.co.
