What We Do With Your Personal Data

  • Process your purchase requests and deliver purchases to you
  • Send marketing and promotional materials (with your consent)
  • Contact you about discounts, deals, or offers
  • Transfer information to service providers needed to fulfill orders
  • Contact you regarding unfinished orders or payment issues

Sharing Your Personal Information

We share your personal information with:

  • Payment processors — Stripe handles payment processing securely (PCI-DSS compliant)
  • Shipping partners — to deliver your purchases
  • Legal and professional advisors — attorneys, consultants, IT service providers
  • Law enforcement — when required by law or to protect our rights
  • Analytics providers — such as Google Analytics

Third-party providers only collect, use, and disclose your information to the extent necessary to perform their services.

Information Security

To protect your personal information, we take reasonable precautions and follow industry best practices to make sure it is not inappropriately lost, misused, accessed, disclosed, altered, or destroyed.

Payment information is processed securely through Stripe using secure socket layer technology (SSL) and is PCI-DSS compliant.

California Residents Rights (CCPA)

If you are a California resident, you have the following rights:

  • Right to opt-out — Request that we do not sell your personal data. Email support@nutrovia.co.
  • Right to know — Request disclosure of what personal information we have collected, used, shared, or sold about you in the last 12 months.
  • Right to delete — Request deletion of your personal information.
  • Right to non-discrimination — We will not discriminate against you for exercising your rights.

We will respond to requests within 45 calendar days. Contact us at support@nutrovia.co.

European Union Residents Rights (GDPR)

Under GDPR, you have the right to:

  • Request confirmation that we are processing your data
  • Request access to your data
  • Request correction of inaccurate information
  • Request deletion of your information
  • Restrict processing of your information
  • Object to collection and use of your information
  • Request data portability
  • Withdraw consent at any time
  • Lodge a complaint with a supervisory authority

Age of Consent

By using this site, you represent that you are at least the age of majority in your state or province of residence. We do not process any personal data of minors 16 years old or younger.
