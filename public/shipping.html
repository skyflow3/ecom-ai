Shipping Policy

Global Shipping

We offer worldwide shipping to most countries, utilizing the swiftest and most secure delivery methods available.

Shipping & Handling Charges

Starting from just $4.95 (or lower with promotional discounts), our fees ensure your order reaches you promptly and securely. Shipping costs are calculated at checkout based on your location and selected shipping method.

Order Processing Time

Orders are processed swiftly, typically within 24 hours. During peak periods such as holidays or special launches, processing times may vary.

Tracking Your Shipment

To track your order:

  1. Copy your order number, tracking number, or email address.
  2. Visit our tracking page or use the tracking link provided in your shipping confirmation email.
  3. Paste the relevant information and click "Track Order."

Shipping Delays

We cannot be held responsible for shipping delays caused by customs, natural events, carrier transfers, or transportation strikes. Any customs fees or charges incurred are the responsibility of the customer.

Damaged or Lost Items

If your order arrives damaged, please contact us at support@nutrovia.co for a free replacement. Kindly provide pictures of the damaged item or packaging along with your order number.

In the event of a lost item, please contact us so we can help locate your order or arrange a replacement.

International Shipping & Customs

For international orders, customs fees may apply when receiving shipments. These fees are determined by your country's customs regulations and are not within our control. It is important to familiarize yourself with your local customs policies.

If you refuse to accept your package at customs due to high fees, we won't be able to refund that order or reship via another carrier. In this case, the package will be abandoned at customs.
