### Architecture
```
FunnelConfig (backward compatible):
  FunnelStep:
    - legacy: templateId + briefOverrides (single variant)
    - new: variants[] with mode: 'template' | 'block', trafficWeight

  FunnelVariant:
    - mode: 'template' → generateFromTemplate()
    - mode: 'block' → generatePage()
    - trafficWeight: percentage for A/B split

  Router: client-side JS + localStorage for traffic splitting
  Metrics: JSON-file tracking (no DB required)
  Auto-config: AI generates FunnelConfig from product brief alone
```
